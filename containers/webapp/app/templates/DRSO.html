Sea Surface Temperature

SEA SURFACE TEMPERATURE. Below is the sea surface temperature in the last two weeks in the Irish EEZ. SEA SURFACE TEMPERATURE ANOMALY. Below is the sea surface temperature anomaly of the last two weeks. A 40-year (1982-2021) reference baseline climatology from OSTIA is used to determine the SST anomaly.

Chlorophyll

CHLOROPHYLL-a CONCENTRATION AND ANOMALY. Below, on the left-hand side, there is the mass concentration of chlorophyll-a in sea water [mg/m3] from the Atlantic Ocean Colour Bio-Geo-Chemical L4 Satellite Observations (https://doi.org/10.48670/moi-00288). On the right-hand side, there is the chlorophyll-a anomaly determined as the difference between the actual chlorophyll-a concentration and a 60-day running median, ending two weeks before the current image (Tomlinson et al., 2004).
