MARINE OBSERVATORY FOR AQUACULTURE

This web portal seeks to provide oceanographic services for the aquaculture sector. The portal provides access to in-situ observations, forecasts and remote-sensing data products and warnings for Deenish Island (Ireland) and El Campello (Spain).
